#################
isolation_monitor
#################

Isolation monitor types

.. _isolation_monitor-IsolationMeasurement:

| **IsolationMeasurement**: *object* 
|  Results of an isolation measurement
|  properties:
|   **resistance_F_Ohm**: *number* <required>
|    Isolation resistance between DC+/DC- and protective earth in Ohm
|   **voltage_V**: *number* <optional>
|    DC voltage between positive and negative terminal in Volt
|   **voltage_to_earth_l1e_V**: *number* <optional>
|    DC voltage to earth on L1 in Volt
|   **voltage_to_earth_l2e_V**: *number* <optional>
|    DC voltage to earth on L2 in Volt
